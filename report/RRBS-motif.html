RRBS

Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing (RRBS) uses a methylation-insensitive restriction enzyme, usually MspI with the cutting target 5'C^CGG3' sequences (^ is the cutting site), to enrich CpGs. The enrichment undoubtedly causes preference/bias.
