Bisulfite conversion

From the 'methylation' of cytosines in control DNA, we can estimate bisulfite conversion rate ? as $$ \text{conversion rate} = 1-\text{control DNA methylation level} = {{alldata.bsrate_lambda}}\%. $$

The actual estimate of 'methylation level' is the median of methylation of each bin which is robust.

Error rate

The error rate that a base is mis-detected during library preparation, sequencing, and alignment, can be estimated from inconsistent bases mapped to control DNA. From the error rate distribution of 50-bp bins, the average error rate ? is $$ \text{error rate} = \frac{\text{bases different with dominating base}}{\text{total bases}} = {{alldata.err_rate_lambda}}\%. $$

The actual error rate estimate is the median of each bin which is robust.
