Error rate

The (base-level) error rate that a base is mis-detected during library preparation, sequencing, and alignment, can be estimated ? from inconsistent bases mapped to MT genome. From the error rate distribution of 20-bp binning windows, the average error rate is $$ \text{error rate} = \frac{\text{bases different with dominating base}}{\text{total read bases}} = 7.03 \%. $$

Mutation rate is much smaller than error rate in bisulfite-seq samples.

RRBS

Reduced Representation Bisulfite Sequencing (RRBS) uses a methylation-insensitive restriction enzyme, usually MspI with the cutting target 5'CCGG3' sequences, to enrich CpG-rich sequences.

RRBS CpGs summary
