Bases of each kind of CIGAR? status

CIGAR(Compact Idiosyncratic Gapped Alignment Report) is reported by aligner,
  • M: alignment match
  • I: insertion
  • D: deletion
  • N: skipped region
  • S: soft clipping
  • H: hard clipping
  • P: padding
  • =: sequence match
  • X: sequence mismatch
  • B: backwards skip
